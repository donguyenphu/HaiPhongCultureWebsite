People

Hai Phong people are famous for heartwarming welcome, hard-working and also daring to do what they talked.

When foreign visitors come to The Harbour City, they will receive warm welcome form our people, and also eager guides to every corners of Hai Phong City.

Song

When coming to Hai Phong city, everyone will remember the song Tôi người Hải Phòng , the song has an overall view of Hai Phong, as known as "Flamboyant City" , from history to people and so on.

The song was finished in April 20,1972 , by artist Xuan Binh. The song was opened to Voice of Vietnam radio waves. The song was considered as a memory, a big love to The Harbour City, After 3 years, the author of this song sang to people in Hai Phong.
